THE SOLUTION IMPLEMENTED

To address the usability challenges, I designed a user-friendly food ordering app that allows users to seamlessly place orders from their favorite restaurant. The app features a simplified user flow, clear navigation, and smart restaurant filtering—making it easier for users to find and order exactly what they want.

With an intuitive interface and streamlined checkout process, users can now browse menus, customize meals, and complete orders in just a few taps. Once an order is placed, it is delivered directly to their doorstep within a short and reliable delivery window.

This solution not only improves the overall user experience but also restores confidence in the platform’s ability to meet their everyday food needs efficiently.
